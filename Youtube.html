Facts & FAQs about our Channel

  • Kitchen Alchemy was created in November 2023. The channel was created alongside the Brand in order to produce content about culinary recipes. Kitchenware used in the content produced was from the brand, and is used in order to promote it and show it being at work in the kitchen.

  • Kitchen Alchemy was created due to a School Project. It was first planned in the first quarter of the class year, while the website and youtube channel along with the branding were created in the second quarter of the class year.

  • A Cooking-Based Channel was created due to the passion of all the members for cooking.

  • The members of Kitchen Alchemy are Miguel Antonio Mahilum, Cliff Jaiden Pasco, Leugim Gabriel Panganiban, Cedric Lenard Basas, Clyde Reyes, and John Lego Losuegro
